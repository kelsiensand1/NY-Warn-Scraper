By Kelsie Sandoval
Published May 23, 2019 6:00 PM

Covid-19 has caused devastating layoffs across the country. And New York state is no exception. Although anecdotes about people losing their jobs in the restaurant industry are powerful, there is also data to dovetail those claims. 2018 midterms.
